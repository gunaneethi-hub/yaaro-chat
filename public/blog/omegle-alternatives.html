Top Anonymous Chat Apps Like Omegle

Omegle was the internet's favorite place to meet strangers. For over 14 years, millions of people logged on every day just to talk to someone random — no names, no profiles, no history. Just two strangers and a conversation.

Then in November 2023, Omegle shut down forever.

The founder left a long note explaining why. But millions of users were left with one question: where do we go now?

If you're looking for the best anonymous chat apps like Omegle in 2026, you've come to the right place. We've tested every major alternative and put together this list so you don't have to.

What Made Omegle So Popular?

Before jumping into alternatives, it's worth understanding what made Omegle special:

  • Zero sign-up — open the site and start chatting
  • 100% anonymous — no names, no profiles
  • Random matching — you never know who you'll meet
  • Free forever — no premium walls
  • Instant connection — no waiting around

The best Omegle alternatives in 2026 carry all of these qualities forward. Here's our complete list.

Chatroulette is the original video chat roulette — older than Omegle in some ways. It's primarily video-based and has improved its content moderation significantly over the years. If you want face-to-face random conversations, Chatroulette is a solid option.

4. Chathub — Clean Interface

Chathub offers random video and text chat with filtering options. You can select your language and gender preferences before starting, which improves matching quality. It's a reliable option with an active user base.

5. Chatki

Chatki focuses on video chat with strangers and has a straightforward design. It works without registration and offers basic filtering options. A decent option if you prefer video over text.

How to Stay Safe on Anonymous Chat Apps

Anonymous chat can be a great way to meet people — but it's worth following a few simple rules:

  • Never share personal information — no full name, phone number, or address
  • Trust your instincts — if a conversation feels wrong, skip or disconnect immediately
  • Use platforms with reporting tools — good apps let you flag bad actors
  • Prefer text over video — video calls can be recorded without your knowledge
